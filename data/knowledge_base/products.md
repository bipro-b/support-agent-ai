# Products & Stock

## Product availability
Stock levels shown on a product page are updated in real time. "Low stock" means
fewer than 10 units remain. An item in your cart is not reserved until you
complete checkout, so popular items can sell out while in your cart.

## Restocks and back-in-stock alerts
For out-of-stock items, click "Notify me" on the product page to get an email
when it is restocked. We cannot guarantee a restock date, and not every item is
restocked. Notification does not reserve the item.

## Sizing and fit
Each product page has a size guide linked near the size selector. Sizes follow
standard regional measurements. If an item runs small or large, it is noted in
the product description under "Fit notes".

## Warranty
Electronics carry a 1-year limited manufacturer warranty covering defects in
materials and workmanship. The warranty does not cover accidental damage, water
damage, or normal wear. Keep your receipt as proof of purchase.

## Price matching
We do not offer price matching against other retailers. However, if an item you
bought goes on sale within 7 days of your purchase, contact support and we refund
the difference once per item.

## Gift cards
Gift cards are delivered by email and never expire. They can be used on any order
but cannot be redeemed for cash or combined with a promo code on the same order.
