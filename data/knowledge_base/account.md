# Account & Security

## Creating an account
You can create an account with an email and password, or sign up with Google or
Apple. An account is not required to check out — guest checkout is available —
but an account lets you track orders and save addresses.

## Resetting your password
Click "Forgot password" on the login page and enter your email. We send a reset
link that is valid for 60 minutes. If you do not see the email, check your spam
folder. The link can only be used once.

## Changing your email address
Go to "Account settings" and update your email. For security, we send a
confirmation link to the new address; the change takes effect only after you
click it. Your old email keeps working until then.

## Two-factor authentication
We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) under "Account
settings" > "Security". You can use an authenticator app or SMS codes. If you
lose access to your 2FA method, support can verify your identity and disable it.

## Deleting your account
You can request account deletion under "Account settings" > "Privacy". Deletion
is permanent and removes your order history after a 30-day grace period. Any
open orders or pending refunds must be resolved before deletion completes.

## Suspicious activity
If you notice orders or logins you do not recognize, change your password
immediately and contact support. We never ask for your full password or card
number by email or phone.
