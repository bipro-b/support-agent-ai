# Payments & Billing

## Accepted payment methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
We do not accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, or cryptocurrency.

## When you are charged
Your card is authorized when you place the order but only charged when the order
ships. For pre-order items, you are charged at the time of purchase, not at
shipment.

## Failed or declined payments
If a payment is declined, the order is not placed. Common causes are an expired
card, insufficient funds, or a billing address that does not match your bank
records. You can retry with a different payment method from the checkout page.

## Promo codes and discounts
Enter promo codes in the "Discount" field at checkout. Only one promo code can be
used per order, and promo codes cannot be combined with gift cards on the same
order. Codes are case-sensitive.

## Double charges
A pending authorization may briefly appear alongside the final charge. This is
not a double charge; the authorization drops off within 3-5 business days. If two
identical settled charges appear, contact support with both transaction IDs.

## Invoices and receipts
A receipt is emailed after each order ships. You can download a detailed invoice
from "My Orders" by selecting an order and clicking "Download invoice".
