# Shipping & Delivery

## Delivery timeframes
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the country. Express shipping
takes 1-2 business days. Orders placed after 2:00 PM are processed the next
business day. We do not ship on weekends or public holidays.

## Shipping costs
Standard shipping is free for orders over 50 USD. For orders under 50 USD,
standard shipping costs 4.99 USD. Express shipping is a flat 12.99 USD
regardless of order value.

## International shipping
We ship to over 40 countries. International delivery takes 7-14 business days.
Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the customer and are
not included in the shipping cost.

## Tracking your order
Once your order ships, you receive a tracking number by email. You can also find
tracking under "My Orders" in your account. Tracking updates can take up to 24
hours to appear after the label is created.

## Lost or delayed packages
If your package has not arrived within 3 business days of the estimated delivery
date, contact support. For packages marked delivered but not received, we open an
investigation with the carrier, which takes 5-7 business days to resolve.
