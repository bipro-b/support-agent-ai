# Returns & Refunds

## Return window
You can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items
must be unused, in their original packaging, and with tags attached. Final-sale
items and gift cards cannot be returned.

## How to start a return
Go to "My Orders", select the item, and choose "Return or exchange". You will
receive a prepaid return label by email. Pack the item securely and drop it off
at any partner carrier location within 14 days of generating the label.

## Refund processing time
Once we receive and inspect your return, we issue the refund within 3-5 business
days. Refunds go back to the original payment method. Your bank may take an
additional 5-10 business days to post the refund to your account.

## Return shipping costs
Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. For change-of-mind
returns, a flat 5 USD return shipping fee is deducted from your refund.

## Exchanges
To exchange for a different size or color, start a return and place a new order
for the replacement item. We do not do direct swaps because inventory moves
quickly and we cannot hold an item during transit.

## Damaged or defective items
If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact support within 7 days with a
photo. We send a free replacement or a full refund including any shipping paid,
and you do not need to return the damaged item in most cases.
